HOW TO USE WORD COUNTER?

Word counter is used to count words in a text entered by the user. Our platform provides additional feature such as counting vowels,consonants,sentences etc. in oder to use the word counter, just simply enter the text of which you want to count the given features and all the things will be automatically counted.
